About Me

My name is Rachel Sakles. Previous background was in sciences/ pre-nursing. I have my associates degree in liberal arts, but then I decided programming was what I wanted to do.

I am a full stack web developer that graduated from University of Arizona Full Stack bootcamp. My previous education was about helping people. I want to apply that passion through programming. Recently acquired new skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Node.js. Passionate about engaging my audience, and creating meaningful web applications that can make using applications more convenient. I want to create an overall better experience on the web. I worked on a team of 4, where we developed a single page mobile device friendly app that allowed our user to enter a city, and retrieve specific current data back. I have also created an album marketplace where you can buy and sell albums. I want to be one step ahead. To be successful you must think of what your audience wants and needs before they do. I am excited to leverage my knowledge to create a superior and convenient experience on the web.
